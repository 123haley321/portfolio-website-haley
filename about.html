About Me

My Story

I am UX Designer based in Houston, Texas. I create beautiful designs that make people's lives a little bit easier. I am passionate about designing things that make the product you are using both useful and enjoyable, to make the lives of others at least a little bit less stressful. My journey actually began in the field of Finance. While working at a bank, I collaborated with the technology team and knew I needed a career where collaboration and finding solutions were they key components of the job. When I came across UX Design, I was instantly hooked. It was everything that I was looking for in a career. Helping people through creating designs that provided innovative solutions through the collaboration of multiple teams. I had found the career for me. Outside of my life as a UX Designer, I love to explore the world, finding the beauty in nature and the culture of others, enjoying the talents of others, including concerts, art shows theatre and sculpting polymer clay.
